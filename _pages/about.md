I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Cambridge, currently working under the supervision of Jamie Vicary. Before this, I did my master's degree in mathematics at the University of Oxford. My research is in theoretical computer science, particularly in algebraic and type-theoretic approaches to higher category theory.

In my spare time, I participate in theatre and singing. I recently joined the National Youth Theatre, and the chapel choir of Christ's College, Cambridge.
